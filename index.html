Carbonate Grains

Clastic rocks form from fragments of pre-existing rocks

  • Form outside the basin
  • Carbonate rocks form through biochemical processes
  • Form within the basin
  • Depends on chemistry of water, temperature of water, biological activity

Carbonate rocks occur from cambrian; Pre cambrian mostly dolomite with algae stromatolite.
